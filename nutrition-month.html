Sebaste Celebrates Nutrition Month 2024 with a Focus on Healthy and Affordable Eating

In celebration of Nutrition Month last July 2024, Sebaste Community Hospital launched a campaign centered on the theme: "Healthy Diet Gawing Affordable for All!"

Throughout the month, various activities were conducted to promote proper nutrition and raise awareness of its importance to both physical and mental health. These included feeding programs in barangays, healthy cooking demos, school-based contests, and health forums about accessible food choices. One of the highlights was the integration of mental wellness discussions in relation to proper diet. Local nutritionists shared how healthy eating habits can reduce anxiety and support emotional balance — especially among youth and the elderly. With active participation from schools, barangay health workers, and families, Sebaste's Nutrition Month proved that with the right mindset and support, health can be both a personal and community achievement. Stay tuned for more health programs and services in Sebaste as we continue to promote a healthier, stronger, and more informed community.
