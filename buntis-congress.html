Sebaste Successfully Holds Buntis Congress 2022: Empowering Expecting and Future Moms

Sebaste, Antique — The Municipality of Sebaste held the Buntis Congress on November 9, 2022, a special health event dedicated to maternal care and wellness. This initiative provided pregnant and would-be mothers with a rare opportunity to engage directly with medical professionals and gain access to free health services in a supportive setting.

Organized by the Sebaste Rural Health Unit in coordination with the Sebaste Community Hospital and barangay health workers, the event promoted maternal education and reinforced the value of early prenatal care.

The program began at 8:00 AM at a central venue, where participants gathered for informative sessions, checkups, and community bonding. Beyond learning sessions, mothers were treated to enjoyable activities and essential services aimed at supporting a safe and healthy pregnancy journey.

  • Free Registration
  • Exciting Giveaways & Gifts for Early Birds
  • Free PhilHealth Enrollment Assistance
  • Access to Health Services and Commodities
  • Free Tetanus Toxoid Vaccines for indigent pregnant women
  • Free Snacks and Refreshments

Transportation was arranged through Barangay Health Workers (BHWs), ensuring that participants from even the remotest areas could attend with ease.

Events like the Buntis Congress serve as a testament to Sebaste’s commitment to grassroots healthcare. By focusing on maternal health, the local government helps ensure safer pregnancies and healthier babies—paving the way for a stronger future generation.
