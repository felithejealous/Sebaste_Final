Sebaste Launches Health Summit 2023: Focus on Lifestyle Diseases and Lung Health

Sebaste, Antique — In an effort to combat the rising tide of lifestyle-related illnesses, the municipality of Sebaste held Health Summit 2023 on [insert date here] at the Sebaste Covered Gym. The event placed a spotlight on non-communicable diseases (NCDs)—the leading cause of death in the area—and emphasized the importance of taking active steps toward a healthier lifestyle.

One of the highlights of the summit was "ZUMBABIDA," a community Zumba session that encouraged residents to get moving and embrace physical activity as part of their daily routine. The upbeat activity drew enthusiastic participation from health workers, residents, and community leaders alike.

Aside from promoting physical fitness, the summit also addressed the growing concern around tuberculosis (TB). Health officials warned about the disease’s increasing resistance to treatment, largely due to poor compliance with medication. They stressed the importance of lung health and boosting immunity, especially in vulnerable individuals.

To further support the community’s wellness, the event offered free Flu and Pneumonia vaccinations to attendees. Residents were advised to come early due to limited vaccine supplies.

Health workers and volunteers from the Rural Health Unit (RHU) were on-site to assist and ensure a smooth flow of activities. The summit marked another step forward in the municipality’s mission to create a healthier, more informed Sebaste.
