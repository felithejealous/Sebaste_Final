Sebaste Opens Health Summit 2023: A Three-Day Drive Toward Inclusive and Responsive Healthcare

Sebaste, Antique — The Municipality of Sebaste officially launched Health Summit 2023 on December 13, 2023, at the Sebaste Covered Gym. With the theme "Sebaste: Keeping Apace with the Challenges of UHC Towards Inclusive and Responsive Health System," the summit aims to address pressing health concerns and promote Universal Health Care (UHC) awareness among the local population.

The three-day event commenced with a vibrant motorcade, joined by key stakeholders from ten barangay local government units (LGUs). This was immediately followed by an opening program that set the tone for the summit’s focus on health empowerment and community engagement.

Running from December 13 to 15, the summit features a range of services and activities, including:

  • Health Forums discussing local health challenges
  • Free vaccinations: bOPV (oral polio vaccine), Influenza, and Pneumonia
  • Free screenings for Diabetes and Hypertension
  • Medical consultations provided at no cost

Residents are encouraged to participate and take advantage of the services offered. The event emphasizes not only treatment but also awareness and prevention—empowering Sebastehanons to take control of their health.

“Don’t miss this chance to be informed and proactive about your health,” organizers reminded attendees.
