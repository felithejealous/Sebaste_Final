Advisory No. 2: Continuation of Free Consultation for Children

June 19, 2025

We are pleased to inform the community of Sebaste that free consultations for children aged 0-5 years will continue at Sebaste Community Hospital. This is in support of the health of our young ones.

Details:

  • Who is qualified: All children from 0 to 5 years old.
  • When: Every Monday to Friday, from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
  • Where: Pediatric Outpatient Department, Sebaste Community Hospital.

Please bring a birth certificate or any proof of the child's age.
