Mental Health Services & Awareness

Sebaste Community Hospital continues its commitment to promoting mental wellness by offering comprehensive mental health services throughout the year 2025. Our programs focus on raising awareness, providing counseling, and facilitating support groups to help community members manage mental health challenges effectively.

Understanding the importance of mental health in overall well-being, our trained professionals conduct seminars, workshops, and outreach activities. These initiatives educate the public on recognizing early signs of mental health conditions and encourage seeking timely help without stigma.

Additionally, the hospital provides accessible mental health consultations, psychological assessments, and therapy sessions tailored to individual needs. Collaboration with barangay health workers ensures that these services reach even the most remote areas of Sebaste, promoting inclusive care for all.

We believe that mental health awareness and support are vital components of a healthy community, and Sebaste Community Hospital is dedicated to continuously expanding and improving these services.
